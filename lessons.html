Teaching Philosophy

I believe in the freedom that comes from learning and knowing your instrument through and through. The term "fret-board freedom" comes from the ability to play on the guitar anything heard, read, or imagined.

I challenge my students to learn to how to read music and join the world of musicians that speak the same musical language. In this way, we can overcome the language barrier and communicate, connect, and collaborate with all musicians.
